# Playback Control

## Global Playback Control

### Floating Play Button
- Drag to a suitable position
- Click to show playback control panel
- **Long press** to toggle play/pause

### Playback Control Panel
- Display current playing content
- Provide richer playback controls: previous program, next program
- Clear playlist

## Play Later

> Play Later is a PRO member feature, subscription required

### Add to Play Later
- Click the "+" button next to the radio
- Select "Add to Play Later"

### Manage Play Later List
- View Play Later content
- Delete unwanted content
